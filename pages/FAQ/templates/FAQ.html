FAQ'S

GiveByClick was established out of an ideology to create a better world and help a population that needs help, in a unique way and by actively using a donation platform.GiveByClick is a non-profit organization, our main goal is to collect donations.

The organization collects the donations and transfers them to relevant organizations that distribute the products collected to the needy throughout the country. GiveByClick is in constant interaction with organizations around the country for assistance.

You can choose from three types of donations:

1. Donate a sum of money of your choice.

2. Food packages.

3. Clothes and products for the home.

Poverty is an important issue that touches the hearts of many of us. We are interested in giving the platform to help anyone who needs help, out of a desire to live in a better world.

We invite you to take part in fundraising events that take place from time to time. In this way you can enjoy different and varied events and also donate to the needy.

Donations can only be made by credit card.
